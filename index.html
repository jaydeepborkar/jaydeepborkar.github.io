Research Interests

Adversarial Machine Learning

Studying and making classifiers robust to different types of adversarial attacks, understanding from where and how do these adversarial examples stem from, accuracy vs robustness tradeoff.

Machine Learning Bias & Fairness

Understanding how classifiers get biased, mitigating the bias.

Natural Language Processing

Studying and designing NLP systems that are fair and robust (to adversarial attacks).

Privacy-Preserving ML

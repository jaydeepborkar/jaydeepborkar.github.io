Hi! I'm Jaydeep. I'm a senior undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at Savitribai Phule Pune University, India. My research and application interests fall in Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing. I'm also very passionate about interdisciplinary research. Living in India, where 22 different languages are spoken with 720 different dialects, I'm interested in building Natural Language Processing based solutions to various problems.

In my free time, I often listen to garbas (I've won prizes as a garba player), write, read, volunteer, or I can be easily spotted on the streets of Pune trying out various cafes and restaurants.

If you want to hire me or collaborate on a research, want a copy of my CV, discuss any interesting idea, share a funny meme, or invite me over a cup of coffee, please contact me.

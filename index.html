Hi! I'm a final year undergraduate student in Computer Engineering at Savitribai Phule Pune University. My research and application areas fall in the field of Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning. I aim to build systems through which humans and computers can talk to each other in ways that are natural, and not awkward. My current research and learning areas span across Tensor methods in Machine Learning, building state-of-the-art Reading Comprehension Systems that have a true understanding of the language, understanding Adversarial Examples in Machine Learning and making classifiers robust to different kinds of adversarial attacks, as well as Ethics and Biases in AI. I believe in robust, fair, and more responsible AI.

Living in India, where 22 different languages are spoken with 720 different dialects, I'm interested in building Natural Language Processing based solutions to various social problems, and I'm currently researching on that.

In my free time, I often listen to garbas (I've won prizes as a garba player), write, read, volunteer, or I can be easily spotted on the streets of Pune trying out various cafes and restaurants.
